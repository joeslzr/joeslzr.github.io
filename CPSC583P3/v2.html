Trump Approval Rate

This variation is a line graph showing the percentage of "Yes" responses over time to the corresponding question category selected. My aim with the color choices was to leverage the colors we are familiar with respect to America. I've chosen to set the curve as "curveLinear" in this iteration for two reasons. First is that line graphs of this nature are sort of the 'standard' thus may be less jarring for the audience compared to the first iteration. Secondly, the interpolation between data points, atleast to me, is more expressive when showing changes over time
