A4: Vibe Cap

Abstract

The objective of this assignment was to explore wearable interaces that communicates the concept of hygge.

Hygge - A Danish word meaning the quality of coziness and contentment that comes from enjoying the simple things in life

For me, one of the most important aspect of creating Hygge is ambiance. Or more colloquially, Good Vibes. That being said, Good vibes is purely subjective and can differ based on the person, context, time of day, etc. Thus, the Vibe Cap - the cap that lets you choose your vibe and plays good vibes when you lean back and relax.

• Wearable Tui
• Stylish! (sans the wires)
• Easy to use!
• Plays music when you relax
• Music selected via the Vibe Meter
