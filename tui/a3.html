A3: "No Phones at the Table!" Jar

Abstract

The objective of this assignment was to explore physical output by physicalizing personal data. Swear jars are classic device used to visualize and discourage bad habits. Instead of swearing, my partner and I decided to tackle a more modern issue: using cellphones at the dinner table. Our "No Phones at the Table!" Jar detects when a phone is tilted (i.e. picked up) and activates an apparatus which slowly pushes a preset amount of coins into a jar. The noise of the coins falling serves to remind the offender to put down his phone. If all of the coins have fallen, an angry buzzer sounds as a final warning.
