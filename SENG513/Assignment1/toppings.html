Top it off

No bowl of Ramen is complete without its toppings. Toppings can range from any from classics like Chashu Pork, to regional/local vegetables. The choice is yours with many shops often allowing you to customize the toppings of your Ramen so that no two bowls are completely the same. Here are some classic toppings

  • Chashu

    A standard toping on any bowl of ramen - Fatty cuts of roasted/braised pork

  • Menma

    Preserved Bamboo Shoots - add a nice chewy bite to your bowl

  • Ajitsuke Tamago

    An incredibily popular topping - softboiled egg marinated in some mix of soysauce, mirin, and other seasonings

  • Narutomaki

    Whtie fishcake with a pink spiral design on it

  • Corn

    Yellow corn kernals often paired with Miso or Shio ramen

  • Butter

    A small knob of butter adds a depth of flavor and creaminess

  • Nori

    Squares of dried seaweed
